How signing in works

GoMooMoo uses passwordless sign-in. On the sign-in screen, enter your email address and tap send — we email you a 6-digit code. Enter the code to sign in. There is no password to remember.

Common questions

  • I did not get my code. Check your spam folder, wait a few moments, then tap resend. Make sure the email address is spelled correctly.
  • How do I connect with my partner? Share your couple invite from within the app so the two of you are linked.
  • How do I delete my account or data? Leave your couple or delete your account in the app, or email us and we will remove your data.
